What We Do

Education

Parivartan Education Foundation is working to help many children, school students and college youth in India. All this for their happy and bright future.

Health

The major focus is to address the healthcare and nutritional needs of the affected people.Also,health awareness programs for adolescent girls and working to improve reproductive health, child health services,and more.Covid-19 helped many people to provide medicine.

Environment

It is the best Indian environmental NGO whose impact is felt across Maharashtra.The organization works to ensure more responsible use of resources.In this organization, they have come together to achieve environmental justice by detoxifying nature. We also do a lot of tree planting.

Access & Infrastructure

A major finding of this organization is the present school. But when the construction of a school with necessary facilities for the students is not seen, schools are built for them and all the necessary help is provided to the students in the school.

Agricultural

Land degradation and unsustainable agricultural practices have reduced productivity, fueling rural poverty. In fact, India's agricultural productivity is among the lowest in the world.

Social Work

The sole purpose of this organization is to advance and disseminate research; To achieve this objective, to carry out urgent tasks to solve various problems faced by the people living in the society.
